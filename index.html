Triplight

TripLight is a financial management tool aimed at fairly distrubting costs for group trips. Triplight stores information about all financial costs of a trip, and equally redistributes the costs to each individaul at the end of a trip to ensure a fair contribution from everyone.

Auto Auction

Auto Auction is an online auctioning platform that allows customers to create auctions and bid on them in a safe and secure manner. Designed for fast and simple sales, each product is shipped out and paid after a pre-defined amount of time listed by the seller.

Covid Tracker

The CovidTracker is a web based tracker of Covid-19, tracking information about current cases, deaths, vaccinations and more. It is updated in real time and allows users to view stats by country or by continent.
